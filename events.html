A look back at key events that have shaped the CEO Roundtable on Cancer’s mission and programs.

2025 Annual Meeting October 28, 2025 · Meridian House, Washington, DC

2025 Annual Meeting: Accelerating Oncology Innovation Through Cross-Sector Collaboration

Leaders from industry, academia, government, and advocacy convened to chart strategies for regulatory modernization, AI integration, and next-generation therapeutics.

01

Opening Remarks

David Reese (Amgen), Sean Khozin (CEORT/PDS)

Featured video with Coach Mike Krzyzewski and Chris Viehbacher on leadership and purpose.

02

Fireside Chat — FDA’s Oncology Agenda

Peter Pisters (MD Anderson) with FDA Commissioner Martin Makary

Regulatory flexibility, adaptive trial designs, real-world data, AI in regulatory submissions, digital twin control datasets.

03

CEO Panel — Leading Transformation

Sunil Verma (AstraZeneca), Christopher Viehbacher (Biogen), Josh Bilenker (Treeline), Kim Rathmell (OSU James)

Organizational transformation, next wave beyond checkpoint inhibitors/ADCs, regulatory modernization, leadership across sectors.

04

Agentic AI in Clinical Trials

Michelle Longmire (Medable), Kiran Patel (J&J), Warren Kibbe (NCI), Matthew Ko (DeepScribe), James Kugler (EMD Digital)

AI-powered trial automation, patient recruitment, agentic AI in trial operations.

05

Innovator Spotlights

Jon McDunn (PDS), Rafael Rosengarten (Genialis), Tony Blau (All4Cure), Aaron Smith (Unlearn), Julie Gerardi (Bioptimus)

Showcasing breakthrough innovation across data science, precision oncology, and AI-driven drug discovery.

06

Aligning Policy, Infrastructure & Capital

Paul Howard (Amicus), Kevin Bugin (Amgen), Robin Prasad (Bold Brain Capital), Andrew Emmett (Pfizer), Kristin Schneeman (Milken FasterCures)

Capacity constraints, evidentiary fragmentation, shared validation environments, trial-network operationalization.

07

Fireside Chat — NCI Blueprint

Sean Khozin with Anthony Letai (NCI Director)

NCI vision, public-private partnerships, precision oncology infrastructure.

08

AI Tumor Assessment Standards

Sean Khozin, Gail Stephens (SAS), Ella Pavlechko (SAS)

autoRECIST demonstration, Total Tumor Burden Index (TTBI), AI-powered volumetric 3D tumor assessment.

09

Endpoints & Evidence

Gideon Blumenthal (Merck), Marjorie Green (Merck), Stuart "Stu" Bailey (EMD Serono), David Spetzler (Caris Life Sciences), Jean-Charles Soria (Amgen)

Redefining surrogate endpoints, MRD/ctDNA validation, precision regulation, confirmatory trial requirements.

10

Next-Generation Immunotherapy

Zhen Su (Marengo), James Gulley (NCI), Sushil Patel (Replimune), Christian Hinrichs (Rutgers), Alicia Zhou (Cancer Research Institute)

Oncolytic viruses, advanced cellular therapy (TIL, logic-gated CAR-T, in vivo cell engineering), bispecifics, NK-cell platforms, manufacturing scale, perioperative opportunity.
