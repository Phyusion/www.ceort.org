News & Press

Latest Updates

Stay informed about the latest developments, announcements, and milestones from the CEO Roundtable on Cancer.

July 2025

Two Esteemed Oncology Leaders Join Life Sciences Council

The CEO Roundtable on Cancer appoints Dr. W. Kimryn Rathmell, CEO of The Ohio State University Comprehensive Cancer Center, and Dr. Sunil Verma of AstraZeneca to its Life Sciences Council.

October 2024

CEORT & Paradigm Health Launch PACT Collective

The CEO Roundtable on Cancer and Paradigm Health announce a strategic partnership to launch the PACT Collective, expanding clinical trial access and diversity in underserved communities.

September 2024

Going for Gold Initiative Launches to Advance Equitable Cancer Care

The CEO Roundtable on Cancer launches the Going for Gold initiative, partnering Gold Standard companies with HBCUs and minority-serving institutions to promote health equity.

2024

Project Data Sphere Surpasses 250,000 Patient Records

The open-access clinical trial data platform reaches a landmark milestone, now hosting over 252 clinical trials and more than 250,000 de-identified patient records for cancer researchers worldwide.

February 2024

2024 Annual Meeting Highlights Cancer Moonshot Engagement

The CEO Roundtable Partnership Summit highlights engagement with the Cancer Moonshot, Cancer Stage Shifting Initiative, and the ACE Cohort in Clinical Trials program.

2024

5 New Companies Join CEORT Mission to Reduce Cancer's Toll

Elligo Health Research, Black Diamond Therapeutics, Change Healthcare, Jasper Health, and B&C International join the CEO Roundtable on Cancer as new member organizations.
