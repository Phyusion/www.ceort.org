Your Gift Makes a Difference

The CEO Roundtable on Cancer is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to making continual progress toward the elimination of cancer. Every dollar you give directly supports our core initiatives:

CEO Cancer Gold Standard™

Helping employers create healthier workplaces through evidence-based cancer prevention, screening, and care programs.

Project Data Sphere®

Providing open access to de-identified patient-level cancer data from 252+ clinical trials, accelerating research worldwide.

Going for Gold

Advancing health equity by partnering with HBCUs, HSIs, and minority-serving institutions to reduce the burden of cancer in underserved communities.

Tax-Deductible Giving

The CEO Roundtable on Cancer, Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation is tax-deductible to the fullest extent permitted by law. Our EIN (Tax ID) is 65-1230448. You will receive a receipt for your records after your gift is processed.
