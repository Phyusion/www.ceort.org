Our Mission

Making Progress Toward the Elimination of Cancer

Founded at the request of President George H.W. Bush, we challenge corporate leaders to do something bold and venturesome about cancer within their own corporate families and beyond.

What We Do

Our Key Initiatives

Through a portfolio of bold programs, we drive measurable progress across cancer prevention, research, and equitable access.

CEO Cancer Gold Standard™

A workplace wellness accreditation program encouraging organizations to reduce cancer risk, promote early detection, and ensure access to quality care for employees and families.

Learn more →

Project Data Sphere®

An open-access platform for sharing de-identified, patient-level clinical trial data—encompassing 252+ trials and 250,000+ patient records—to accelerate cancer research.

Learn more →

PACT Collective

The Partnership for Access, Collaboration, and Trust expands clinical trial access and diversity in underserved communities through strategic collaboration with Paradigm Health.

Learn more →

Featured Initiative

Going for Gold

Partnering Gold Standard companies with Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCUs) and minority-serving institutions to champion cancer prevention, diagnosis, and quality treatment in communities disproportionately affected by cancer.

  • Capacity to impact 20+ million lives within 7–10 years
  • Focus on health equity, education, navigation, and access
  • Partnerships with HBCUs and minority-serving institutions
  • Connected to the White House Cancer Moonshot initiative
